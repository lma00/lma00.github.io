Chewie Cookies

Description

These chewie cookies are wonderful for a Star Wars Marathon! Makes 3 dozen cookies!

Ingredients

  • 2.25 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 0.5 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Procedure

  1. Mix the flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon in a bowl and stir with a wooden spoon until well mixed. When done, set it aside.
  2. Use an electric mixer set on high speed to beat the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar together in another bowl for 3 minutes or until well blended and creamy.
  3. Beat in the eggs and vanilla extract into the previous bowl and add the flour mixture and stir with wooden spoon until blended. Then, stir in the chocolate chips.
  4. Using a gingerbread man cookie cutter, make around 36 cookies onto a baking sheet.
  5. Put in oven and bake until golden brown for about 10 minutes.
  6. Remove the baking sheets from oven and use a sptatula to lift the cookies off and place on cooling racks. Enjoy!
